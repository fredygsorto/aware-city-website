About Us

Introducing "AwareCity," our mobile app designed to assist the homeless community in New York. It includes three tabs: Home, Map, and Charity. The Home tab offers Hotlines, FAQs, and an About Us page. The Map displays shelters and food resources on a color-coded map of NYC. Lastly, the Charity tab lists organizations dedicated to helping the community.
